Crows in the ecosystem

Crows are known for their prominent role in gothic horror and bad luck within the practice of astrology. The Crow is a bird in the corvid family. They are characterized by long black feathers and a particularly loud harsh caw. What do they do for us here in the world? They are a natural part of the food chain. They also are useful when it comes to helping keep the world clean, from trash eating to carcasses they keep the environment clean and moving forward.
